want to sponsor a month?

As they always say, it takes a village. We rely on our community partners to fulfill our mission. Sponsorships and donations are always appreciated! We always look for new community partners to join us at our monthly book giveaway events. We have a few different options for sponsors and donors. You can sponsor a book giveaway, provide catering at our events, or sponsor the craft supplies for the events. Contact us for more information on how to get involved!
