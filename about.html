about us

our story &
our mission

We believe in stories. We believe that stories have the power to engage us, to provoke us, to inspire us and ultimately to connect us to one another. And we believe that children of all ages should be exposed to as many different stories as possible. We believe that children should experience stories about people who may look different, feel different, think different, live different. They should experience stories about people from different cultures, religions, family structures, communities, countries, backgrounds, and any other societal construct that creates social barriers between people. And because we believe in the power of stories, we choose to create access to them for children by giving them away for free.
